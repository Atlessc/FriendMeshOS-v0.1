# FriendMesh Risk Register

Status: active Phase 0 register  
Last reviewed: 2026-07-12  
Review cadence: every phase handoff, before any wire/storage/security change, and before release qualification

This register tracks threats, engineering failures, product-safety limits, and maintenance hazards derived from the canonical [`FriendMesh game plan`](../../FRIENDMESH_FULL_IMPLEMENTATION_GAME_PLAN.md) and [`Meshtastic/FriendMesh architecture handbook`](../../MESHTASTIC_CORE_AND_FRIENDMESH_ARCHITECTURE.md). It distinguishes accepted physical limits from unfinished mitigations.

## Scoring and workflow

Likelihood and impact are scored from 1 (rare/minor) through 5 (expected/catastrophic). Initial score is `likelihood × impact`:

- `20-25`: CRITICAL
- `12-19`: HIGH
- `6-11`: MEDIUM
- `1-5`: LOW

Status values:

- `OPEN`: treatment has not yet been evidenced.
- `MITIGATING`: implementation or qualification is underway.
- `MONITOR`: controls exist, but ongoing evidence is required.
- `ACCEPTED LIMIT`: technically impossible or explicitly bounded; product language must preserve the limit.
- `CLOSED`: evidence proves the treatment and a reviewer accepted the residual risk.

Closing a risk requires a committed evidence link and named reviewer. A passing demo, planned test, or unchecked phase bullet does not close it. Critical/high risks block release unless closed or explicitly accepted by the product owner with user-visible limitation language.

## Accepted non-guarantees

These are permanent product limits, not defects to close by optimistic wording:

| Limit ID | Product must never promise | Required user-visible treatment | Related risks |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| LIM-01 | Traffic-analysis resistance or hidden LoRa metadata/existence | Privacy documentation names visible header/radio metadata | RSK-003, RSK-006 |
| LIM-02 | Future confidentiality from a person who still holds the current group key | Security screen distinguishes roster state from cryptographic revocation | RSK-006, RSK-008 |
| LIM-03 | Guaranteed SOS delivery, responder action, or emergency-service dispatch | Best-effort warning, delivery state, and outside-help confirmation | RSK-023, RSK-024 |
| LIM-04 | Guaranteed remote erasure of screenshots, exports, malicious firmware, or permanently offline copies | Vote/commit UI says mandatory best effort for compliant recipients | RSK-030, RSK-039 |
| LIM-05 | Complete history recovery without a retaining reachable replica | `History incomplete` state and bounded sync explanation | RSK-020, RSK-039 |
| LIM-06 | Physical attribution of retired-key transmissions | Observation/signature confidence only; no attacker-location claim | RSK-031 |
| LIM-07 | One-meter arrival accuracy or stationary body-relative direction without suitable sensors | Show accuracy/source and use safe arrival radius/fallback | RSK-028, RSK-029 |
| LIM-08 | Private encrypted groups in licensed/Ham mode | Disable FriendMesh private groups and expose `HAM MODE` | RSK-017 |
| LIM-09 | Hardware-backed at-rest security on unprotected physical hardware | Explain software/PIN protection and physical-control limit | RSK-011, RSK-038 |
